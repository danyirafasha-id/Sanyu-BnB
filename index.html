Our story

A kitchen that feels like home

Sanyu BnB started as a family table before it was ever a restaurant. That's still how we cook — unhurried, generous, and seasoned properly. Everything on the grill is prepared fresh each morning.

Whether you're stopping in for lunch, settling in for a long dinner, or staying the night in one of our guest rooms, the goal is the same: you leave fuller and lighter than you arrived.

  • Charcoal-grilled meats & fresh fish, prepared daily
  • Cozy indoor seating plus a shaded outdoor patio
  • Comfortable guest rooms available for overnight stays
  • Private space for gatherings and celebrations
